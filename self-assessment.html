Scoring Guide

25–30: You're operating with strong leadership habits across the board.

18–24: Solid foundation — pick your weakest area and focus there for 2 weeks.

10–17: You're likely still spending most energy on IC work. Re-read Foundations and People & Culture.

0–9: Start with one section. Pick 3 items from this list and commit to them this week. Progress, not perfection.
