Scoring Guide (67 items total)

55–67: You're operating with strong leadership habits across the board. Keep investing in the areas that still feel weakest.

40–54: Solid foundation — pick your weakest section and focus there for 2 weeks. Most leaders live here for years, and that is fine.

20–39: You're likely still spending most energy on IC work. Re-read Foundations and People & Culture — the shift compounds from there.

0–19: Start with one section. Pick 3 items and commit to them this week. Progress, not perfection.

If you lead in a product company rather than consulting, skip the Consulting Mindset section and adjust your total accordingly (from 67 to 62).

The greatest leader is not the one who does the greatest things, but the one who gets people to do the greatest things.
